Our Story

Cotswold House Co. is bringing classic British decor to North America. It's more about curated pieces and rare finds that tell a story, rather than run of the mill.

Everything is hand-selected and picked with purpose in mind. Born from the rolling hills of the Cotswolds, now rooted in Greenville, South Carolina.

The Cotswolds

The Swans
of Bibury.

Bibury — described by William Morris as "the most beautiful village in England." It's where our story begins.

The honey-stone cottages of Arlington Row, the River Coln winding through the village, and the swans that glide across its waters. This is the world that shapes our eye, our taste, and everything we bring to Greenville.
